Click-Lock Rotation Holder

Mechanical

The Click-Lock Rotation Holder is a 3D smartphone stand model designed for secure portrait and landscape use with integrated support for wired charging.

Status

Finished

Timeline

1 week

Read More Back to Portfolio

Project Overview

The Click-Lock Rotation Holder was designed to provide users with a stable and adjustable platform for holding smartphones in both vertical and horizontal orientations.

The design emphasizes rotational flexibility, secure angle locking, and charging accessibility, making it suitable for use during video calls, filming, or desktop use.

Requirements and Technical Challenges

Dual Orientation Support

The holder must allow the phone to rotate between portrait and landscape modes and securely lock at various angles.

Charging Clearance

The phone must remain fully usable and chargeable while docked in either orientation.

Key challenges included designing a rotational mechanism that is strong yet simple to fabricate and ensuring that the holder geometry allowed for standard charging cables without interference.

Key Features

  • An interlocking radial gear interface allows the holder to snap and lock into discrete rotational angles.
  • Easily switch between vertical and horizontal setups.
  • A tripod-style lower structure ensures stability on flat surfaces.
  • Side clamps ensure the phone stays in place during rotation or adjustment.

Development Process

Phase 1: Requirements Analysis

Analyzed existing products to identify gaps in functionality and design. Established key requirements: dual orientation, charging compatibility, and stability.

Phase 2: Initial modeling

Focused on defining the clamp dimensions to fit a generic smartphone, creating a secure holding mechanism. Developed the radial gear interface to allow for easy rotation and locking.

Final Assembly and Results

The final design consists of a base stand, a vertical support, a rotating joint with gear teeth for locking, and a phone holder frame with sufficient clearance for wired charging. The resulting assembly is robust, versatile, and ready for 3D printing, offering practical usability for a variety of daily phone usage scenarios.
